My opinion on the rating:

If you are a user of Apple products, then the choice is quite simple, take the Apple iPhone 11 Pro Max model if it suits you more in size and needs a top-end camera. If the phone is too big for your palm or you did not plan to spend a lot of money on a new phone, the Apple iPhone 11 will be a good acquisition.

If you are an Android user, then there are several more options. First of all, it’s worth it to decide for yourself whether you will bother with installing custom firmware on Huawei phones, in case Google services stop working on them. If so, then you should look at 5G support, how important it is for you at the moment (it depends on the region where you live, how often you travel and how often you update your phone).

There are a lot of Samsung phones in the ranking, although in essence these are S10 and Note 10 options with and without 5G, then everyone chooses for himself based on the budget.

OnePlus model is probably worth choosing for those who like Apple's approach, but prefer Android. Let me remind you that OnePlus releases two phones a year, one in the summer, the second, with slight improvements, in the fall and support the current version of Android for a long time. In terms of performance and characteristics, some of the best Android phones, as we put out of the rating. For some unknown reason, there is no updated OnePlus 7T Pro model in the rating, it is based on the updated SnapDragon 855 Plus processor (unlike the OnePlus 7 Pro, which is on the “regular” 855).

I personally own Xiaomi Mi 9 (before that was Mi 6), and I am impressed with the growth in photo quality (the processor is always installed top-end). As we can see from the rating, the Xiaomi Mi Note 10 model is higher, because it uses a newer sensor, although the processor is weaker than the Mi 9.

Huawei has three main lines, the top-end (on the camera) P-series, which is usually released in March-April, the top-end (on the processor) Mate series, which usually uses the same sensor as the P-series with some modifications. Well, nowadays, Honor is actively promoting nonsense for young people, offering top-end equipment for a reasonable price for cheap and cheerful. The 2019 Huawei model has 30 in the name and 20 respectively - the 2018 models, which also shoot very well and can be bought at significant discounts.

Oppo is a new dark horse with good photo quality, but I would honestly choose another more famous manufacturer, so that in case of problems with the phone there would be more chances to find a service center nearby.
