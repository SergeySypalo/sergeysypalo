Samsung unveiled the flagship Samsung Galaxy S21 line of camera phones online on January 14, 2021. It consists of 3 models: the base Galaxy S21 5G, Galaxy S21 + 5G and Galaxy S21 Ultra. The main feature of the series, as befits a flagship, is support for the HDR10 + display, 120 Hz refresh rate, 8K shooting at 30fps and improved 5G support. The entire line runs on the Android 11 operating system with the updated One UI 3.1 shell. Samsung did not change its tradition and the European versions of phones are based on their Exynos 2100 processor for the European market, while models based on Qualcomm 888 are supplied for the American market. And now more about each model.

Samsung Galaxy S21 5G

The base model, the smallest in size if a 6.2-inch phone can be called small) There is only one option to choose from - 8GB of RAM and 128/256GB. The battery capacity is 4000 mAh.
