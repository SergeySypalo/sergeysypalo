JVZoo

JVZoo one of the oldest affiliate networks, It is been around since 2001, so almost 20 years! It focuses mostly on digital products such as photo/video editing software and making money online guides.

One of the offers they have is product launches, that’s why you might see some outdated offers for products that were launched in the past.
As in any other affiliate network, you can browse/search for offers in the respective area.
You can filter offers by category and sort by EPC to find the most profitable products to promote.
Marketing materials include classic one-page landing pages with product description/sales copy and call-to-action (usually button) in the end.

Commissions:

Tier 1: 35-50%
Tier 2: 0.10$/sale
Recurring: yes, lifetime
